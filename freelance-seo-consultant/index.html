Diagnosing Root Causes, Not Symptoms

Many SEO audits identify surface problems - missing meta descriptions, images without alt text, slow page speed. Those findings matter, but they are symptoms. My audits go to the root cause level so that every fix we implement creates compounding structural improvements, not temporary cosmetic patches.

Ranking position is a leading indicator, not a business outcome. GEO & AEO - The Next Frontier of Search That Most SEO Consultants Are Missing

Search is undergoing its most significant structural shift since Google replaced directory listings in the early 2000s. Generative AI is reshaping how users find answers - and most businesses are not ready. This is where my specialisation in Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) becomes a decisive competitive advantage for clients who move early.

What Is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO is the discipline of making your content and brand visible within AI-generated answers. When a user asks Google a question in 2025, they increasingly receive an AI Overview - a synthesised answer pulled from multiple sources - before they ever see a traditional blue-link result. The same pattern applies to Perplexity AI, ChatGPT Browse, Bing Copilot, and emerging AI search interfaces. GEO optimization ensures your content is selected, cited, and attributed by these AI systems.

The technical requirements for GEO are distinct from traditional SEO. They involve:

  • Entity-first content architecture - structuring content around named entities (people, organisations, concepts, locations) that AI knowledge graphs can map and reference.
  • Authoritative citation signals - building the E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) signals that AI systems use to determine which sources deserve citation trust.
  • Structured data density - comprehensive schema markup (FAQPage, HowTo, Person, Organisation, Article, Speakable) that gives AI parsers explicit, machine-readable context about your content's meaning and authority.
  • Speakable specification implementation - tagging the specific content sections most likely to be extracted by voice assistants and AI answer panels.
  • Knowledge Panel optimisation - strengthening the entity signals that populate and maintain Google Knowledge Panels for your brand and key personnel.

What Is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AEO focuses on capturing the zero-click real estate at the top of traditional search results - featured snippets, People Also Ask (PAA) boxes, knowledge panels, and voice search answers. While GEO targets AI-generated search interfaces, AEO targets the structured answer positions within Google's existing SERP architecture. Both disciplines share overlapping techniques but require distinct strategic framing.

Effective AEO requires:

  • Question-format content targeting high-volume PAA queries in your niche
  • Concise, direct answer paragraphs (40-60 words) immediately following H2/H3 question headings
  • FAQPage, HowTo, and Speakable schema implementation
  • Structured comparison tables and numbered lists that Google can extract for direct display
  • Conversational keyword research targeting voice search phrasing patterns

I have implemented GEO and AEO strategies across 5+ domains with verifiable featured snippet placements and confirmed AI Overview citations. If your business is not optimised for AI search in 2025, you are already losing visibility to competitors who are. Book an AEO/GEO consultation to understand your current AI search exposure.

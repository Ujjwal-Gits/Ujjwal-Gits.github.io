What Is Local SEO?

Local SEO (Local Search Engine Optimisation) is the practice of optimising your online presence so that your business appears prominently in geographically relevant searches. Unlike traditional SEO which targets global or national rankings, local SEO focuses on capturing customers within a specific city, district, or radius.

The three pillars of local SEO are: your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business), your local citations (business listings on directories like Yelp, Yellow Pages Nepal, and industry-specific platforms), and your on-page geo-signals (location-specific content, schema markup, and NAP consistency). When these three pillars align, Google's local algorithm rewards your business with prominent placement in the Map Pack and local organic results.

My Local SEO Services

Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization

Your Google Business Profile is your digital storefront. I provide end-to-end GBP optimisation including: business category selection, service area configuration, attribute optimisation, photo strategy (exterior, interior, team, products), post scheduling, Q&A management, and verification assistance. A fully optimised GBP signals relevance to Google's local algorithm and dramatically increases your visibility in the Map Pack.

Local Citation Building & NAP Consistency

Citations are mentions of your business name, address, and phone number (NAP) across the web. I build and audit citations on platforms like Yelp, TripAdvisor, Yellow Pages Nepal, and industry directories. NAP inconsistencies - even minor variations like "St." vs "Street" - confuse Google and suppress your local rankings. I ensure 100% NAP consistency across every listing.

Local Keyword Research & Content

I conduct keyword research specifically focused on geo-modified terms - "best dentist in Itahari", "web developer Kathmandu", "plumber near Biratnagar." I then create geo-targeted content - location pages, service area pages, and locally-relevant blog posts - that establish topical authority for your service area.

Review Strategy & Reputation Management

Google reviews are a top-3 local ranking factor. I implement systematic review generation campaigns - including review request templates, QR code strategies, and response management - that build a consistent stream of positive reviews while maintaining compliance with Google's review policies.

Local Schema & Structured Data

I implement LocalBusiness schema markup, GeoCoordinates, OpeningHoursSpecification, and AggregateRating structured data on your website. This gives Google rich context about your business location, hours, and services - improving both your local rankings and your appearance in rich search results.

What Exactly Is a UI/UX Audit?

A UI/UX audit is a rigorous, evidence-based evaluation of a digital product's user interface (UI) and user experience (UX). It goes far beyond subjective opinions about colour schemes or font choices. A proper audit examines interaction patterns, information architecture, cognitive load, accessibility compliance, and conversion funnel efficiency through established frameworks like Nielsen's 10 Usability Heuristics and WCAG 2.2 guidelines.

For Nepali businesses - from e-commerce platforms in Kathmandu to SaaS products in Biratnagar - a professional usability testing audit in Nepal reveals the exact moments where users abandon your site, fail to complete purchases, or simply feel confused. These invisible friction points cost you revenue every single day.

Comprehensive UI/UX Audit Services I Offer

Heuristic Evaluation

I systematically evaluate every screen of your product against Jakob Nielsen's 10 Usability Heuristics - visibility of system status, match between system and real world, user control and freedom, consistency and standards, error prevention, recognition over recall, flexibility and efficiency, aesthetic design, error recovery, and help documentation. Each violation is scored by severity and documented with precise remediation steps.

Mobile Responsiveness Audit

Accessibility & WCAG Compliance Review

I perform both automated and manual accessibility audits against WCAG 2.2 Level AA standards.
